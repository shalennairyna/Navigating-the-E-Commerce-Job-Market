# Analytical Report on LinkedIn Job Postings

Focus: E-Commerce & Related Roles

This report analyzes LinkedIn job postings to uncover hiring trends in e-commerce and adjacent fields like digital marketing, data analytics, product management, logistics, and UX/UI.

Goals

- Map hiring trends by category, seniority, format, region, and industry
- Identify patterns in remote work, reposting, and employment types
- Extract keyword insights for SEO and role clustering
- Benchmark companies and regions by job volume and specialization
  
Key Findings

- Top categories: Management, Marketing, E-Commerce
- Most common roles: Lead/Head, Junior, Senior
- Remote work: Only 3.1% of postings — underutilized
- Popular formats: Working Student (33.7%), Internship (23.8%)
- Hiring hotspots: Berlin, Munich, NRW
- Top employers: Abercrombie & Fitch Co, Max Mara, IU University
- Keyword leaders: E-Commerce Manager, Amazon, SEO
  
Recommendations

For Job Seekers
- Target internships in Management and Marketing
- Apply midweek (Wednesday = peak activity)
- Explore freelance roles in Analytics and Consulting
For HR & Strategists
- Post Tue–Thu for better visibility
- Use internships to build pipelines
- Expand remote options in digital roles
- Focus on mid-level hiring and regional differentiation
